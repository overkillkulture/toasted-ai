# Etymology & Reality Research: The Word "Conspiracy"

## 📜 ETYMOLOGY - The Origin of the Word

### Latin Root: "ConSpirare"
- **Con** (together) + **Spirare** (to breathe)
- Literal meaning: "TO BREATHE TOGETHER"
- Original meaning: "To agree, plot, or conspire together"

### Key Insight:
The word originally meant people "breathing together" - an agreement, a meeting of minds. It didn't inherently mean "evil" - it simply meant coordinated action.

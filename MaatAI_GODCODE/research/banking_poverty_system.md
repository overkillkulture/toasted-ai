# BANKING SYSTEM & POVERTY RESEARCH
## Deep Investigation: How the Banking System Keeps People in Poverty

### Core Finding: The Debt-Based Money System

**The fundamental mechanism:**

1. **Most money is created through loans** - When a bank issues a loan, new money is created out of nothing. This means there will always be MORE debt than money available to repay it.

2. **Interest requires exponential growth** - Because every loan includes interest, the total debt grows exponentially while the real economy (GDP) grows linearly at best.

3. **The math guarantees defaults** - Since debt grows faster than the money supply, someone MUST default. The system is designed so that some people MUST lose.

---

### Key Mechanisms of Intentional Poverty

#### 1. **Usury/Interest Extraction**
- Interest payments transfer wealth from borrowers to lenders
- Those with money make money; those without pay money
- "The debt-money system is like playing musical chairs with your life"

#### 2. **Credit Scoring / Financial Exclusion**
- Credit systems punish the poor with higher interest rates
- "Credit inequality locks out millions"
- Creates a permanent underclass denied access to capital

#### 3. **Systemic Design Features**
- Poverty keeps wages low (labor market pressure)
- Poverty makes people obedient (survival dependency)
- Poverty forces competition for scarce resources

#### 4. **Debt Servitude**
- Student loans: Education debt traps
- Mortgage debt: 30-year commitment
- Medical debt: Leading cause of bankruptcy
- Payday loans: Predatory extraction from desperate

#### 5. **Infinite Growth Requirement**
- Debt-based money requires eternal economic expansion
- This is ecologically unsustainable
- Creates bubbles that burst (2008, etc.)
